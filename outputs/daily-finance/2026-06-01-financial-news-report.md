## Key Financial News

- **US equities reached new all-time highs for the ninth consecutive week.** The S&P 500 closed at 7,599.96 (+0.26%) and the Nasdaq at 27,086.81 (+0.42%), the first-ever close above 27,000. The Dow Jones edged up 46 points to 51,078.88. Tech and Energy were the top two performing S&P 500 sectors. ([Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/markets/stocks/live/stock-market-today-monday-june-1-flat-225422503.html), [TheStreet](https://www.thestreet.com/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-dow-jones-sp-500-nasdaq-updates-june-01-2026))
- **Nvidia surged 6%+** after CEO Jensen Huang unveiled the RTX Spark superchip at Computex 2026 — an Arm-based Windows laptop processor co-developed with Microsoft and MediaTek, pairing a Blackwell GPU with a custom Grace CPU and 128GB unified memory. Dell Technologies jumped 10%+ and HP Inc gained 8%. The announcement signals Nvidia's entry into a $200B CPU market. ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/31/nvidias-new-chip-to-power-fresh-line-of-windows-laptops-by-dell-hp.html), [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-01/nvidia-enters-windows-laptop-market-taking-on-intel-and-amd))
- **WTI crude oil surged to ~$92.42/bbl (+5.8%)**, breaking above $90 after Iran halted communications with the US in protest over Israel's ongoing attacks on Lebanon, sharply elevating geopolitical risk premiums in energy markets. ([TheStreet](https://www.thestreet.com/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-dow-jones-sp-500-nasdaq-updates-june-01-2026))
- **Gold fell 1.90% to ~$4,455/oz** as rising oil prices reinforced inflation concerns, strengthening the case for central banks to keep interest rates elevated for longer.
- **US 10-year Treasury yield rose to ~4.47%**, recovering from three-week lows. Markets are pricing roughly a 46% probability of a Fed rate hike in December 2026, reflecting persistent core inflation pressures and a notable shift from earlier expectations of cuts.
- **ASX 200 rose 0.04% to 8,735.3**, a cautious first session of June following Friday's strong 1.62% rally. Information Technology surged 5.43%, tracking the global AI-chip rally; Healthcare fell 1.68%, the weakest sector. ([IBTimes AU](https://www.ibtimes.com.au/asx-200-modest-gains-economic-data-anticipation-1869828))
- **AUD/USD held at 0.7162**; iron ore was last quoted near $108.82/T (May 29 data), up ~1.53% on the month and ~9.79% year-on-year, with no new June 1 settlement data available.
