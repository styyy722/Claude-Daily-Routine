# Daily Financial News Report Template

Each report should be one page only.

## Required report structure

# Daily Financial News Report – YYYY-MM-DD

## Executive Summary
- 3 bullet points only.
- Focus on the most market-moving developments.
- Avoid unnecessary detail.

## Market Snapshot

Include a concise markdown table covering:
- S&P 500
- Nasdaq
- Dow Jones
- ASX 200, if available
- AUD/USD
- US 10-year yield
- Gold
- Oil
- Iron ore, if available

## Key Financial News

Include 5–7 bullet points covering:
- Global equity markets
- US market movements
- Australian market implications
- Central banks and interest rates
- Bond yields
- FX, especially AUD/USD
- Commodities
- Major company or sector news

## Investment Implications

Include 3 bullet points explaining what the news may mean for markets.

Do not provide personalised financial advice.

## Watchlist for Today

Include 3 bullet points covering what investors should monitor today.

## Style rules

- Professional finance tone.
- Clear and concise.
- Around one page.
- Include source links where possible.
- Do not exaggerate.
- Do not make predictions without evidence.
