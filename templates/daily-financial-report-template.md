# Daily Financial News Report Template

# Daily Financial News Report – YYYY-MM-DD

## Executive Summary
- 3 bullet points only.
- Focus on the most market-moving developments.
- Keep it concise.

## Market Snapshot

Format the Market Snapshot as a **code block table** (wrapped in triple backticks) with columns aligned using spaces. This renders correctly in both Discord and GitHub. Example structure:

```
Asset              | Level           | Change
-------------------|-----------------|-------------------------
S&P 500            | 7,412.60        | +0.12%
Nasdaq 100         | 29,139.00       | +0.29%
Dow Jones          | 49,782.10       | -0.04%
ASX 200            | 8,684.40        | -0.20%
AUD/USD            | 0.7223          | -0.37%
US 10-Yr Yield     | ~4.42%          | ↑ (1-week high)
Gold               | $4,708/oz       | -$10.15
Oil (WTI)          | $101.53/bbl     | +$3.46 (+3.5%)
Iron Ore           | ~$111.42/T      | +0.44%
```

Cover: S&P 500, Nasdaq, Dow Jones, ASX 200 (if available), AUD/USD, US 10-year yield, Gold, Oil, Iron ore (if available).
Use `—` or "N/A" if data is unavailable. Do not use a markdown pipe table.

## Key Financial News

Include 5–7 bullet points covering:
- Global equity markets
- US market movements
- Australian market implications
- Central banks and interest rates
- Bond yields
- FX, especially AUD/USD
- Commodities
- Major company, ETF, or sector news

## Investment Implications

Include 3 bullet points explaining what the news may mean for markets.

Do not provide personalised financial advice.

## Watchlist for Today

Include 3 bullet points covering what investors should monitor today.

## Style Rules

- Professional finance tone.
- Clear and concise.
- Around one page.
- Include source links where possible.
- Do not exaggerate.
- Do not invent data.
- Clearly state when data is unavailable.
